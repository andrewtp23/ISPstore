About Us
Dunder Mifflin Inc. (stock symbol DMI) is a micro-cap regional paper and office supply distributor with an emphasis on servicing small-business clients. With a corporate office in New York City, Dunder Mifflin has branches in Buffalo, Albany, Utica, Scranton, Akron, Camden, Nashua and Yonkers.

Dunder Mifflin Inc. provides its customers quality office and information technology products, furniture, printing values and the expertise required for making informed buying choices. We provide our products and services with a dedication to the highest degree of integrity and quality of customer satisfaction, developing long-term professional relationships with employees that develop pride, creating a stable working environment and company spirit.

About Our Website
How to Use Our Site

  1. First, navigate to our store page here
  2. Next, find an item you would like to purchase. When you have found it, select the quantity. Then, hit "Add to Cart".
  3. Once you have finished shopping, at the cart page, hit "Check Out" to complete your purchase.

How Our Site Works
| Security |
While our storefront page is written in CSS and HTML, our actual store and cart uses a mix of CSS and PHP, and utilizes a server database to create a secure storage of our users' details, as well as our own data.


| Implementation |
Our cart system utilizes session cookies in order to streamline our users' experience, as well as remove processing load from our databases, resulting in a fast, efficient checkout system. When the user submits their order, only then is it committed to our database, where it is securely stored until the order is filled by us.


| Mobility |
Our website is fully mobile-compatible, and will scale to fit on any mobile device. You can order paper on the go!
